**Name:** Rosohna (previously Ghor Dranas)<br>
**Location:** Xhorhas, Wildemount<br>
**Affiliation:** Kryn Dynasty<br>
**Military:** Aurora Watch<br>
**Population:** Drow, Goblin<br>
**Districts:** The Coronas, The Firmaments, The Gallimaufry, The Ghostlands, The Shadowshire<br>
**Colors:** Purple/green

A vast metropolis in almost perpetual night, alight with thousands of small, green, sparkling arcane lanterns. The city's buildings, some of which are repurposed ruins that have been built upon and refurbished, are made of stone, metal, and dark clay. Others, especially closer to the center of the city, appear to be similar to an "elvish domicile design that is familiar yet unfamiliar, in some ways slender and jagged, but beautiful and sleek." The extended wards of the city are more patchwork and scattered, and they extend from the city center in an "onion-layer type layout, circular in the center and more oblong", becoming progressively wider in shape and more diverse in population as one travels outward. Each district is surrounded by large 15-foot barriers.
### Points of Interest:
- Lucid Bastion: court of the ruling clans of the Kryn Dynasty and home to the Bright Queen
- Marble Tomes Conservatory: hall of higher learning
- Firmaments: luxurious city center and place of higher learning
- Coronas: poor areas on the western side of the city.
- Ghostlands: areas beyond the walls of the Coronas
