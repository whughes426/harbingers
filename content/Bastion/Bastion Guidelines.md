 ### Bastion Rules
- Each PC gets two basic rooms (no special benefits) and 2 special facilities.
- Total of 6 special facilities per PC (level 5, 9, 13, 17)How to publish Obsidian notes with Quartz on GitHub Pages
- Can change one special facility each time they level up.
- When PCs are not present, or when "Maintenance" Command is issued, Bastion Event occurs (see table) every 7 days.

### Commands
1. Maintain Order:  applies to entire stronghold
2. Craft
3. Empower
4. Harvest
5. Recruit
6. Research
7. Trade
### Special Facilities (available at Level 5)
1. Arcane Study (craft)
2. Armory (trade)
3. Barracks (recruit)
4. Garden (harvest)
5. Library (research)
6. Sanctuary (craft)
7. Smithy (craft)
8. Storehouse (trade)
9. Workshop (craft)

### Can include
1. Closets
2. Corridors, ramps, staircases
3. Defensive walls: 20 feet high (extra cost)
4. Doors and windows (locked doors, secret doors, portcullis)
