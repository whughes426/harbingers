### Quick Facts
- **Type:** Prime Deity
- **Title:** The Moonweaver
- **Province:** Illusion, Love, Moonlight, Night, Tides
- **Symbol:** Crescent moon turned upward, strung like a bow
- **Gender:** Feminine
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Good
- **Home plane:** Arborea
- **Associated Domains:** Arcana, Moon, Nature, Trickery, Twilight

Widely worshiped within halfling and elven cultures, she is largely considered to be the deity of love and protector of the trysts of lovers. Those who work in secrecy and trickery often ask for her blessing.
