### Quick Facts
- **Type:** Prime Deity
- **Title:** The Archheart, Parent of All Elves
- **Province:** Art, Beauty, Elves
- **Symbol:** Two crested moons facing each other atop a four-pointed star
- **Gender:** Non-binary
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Good
- **Home plane:** Arborea
- **Worshipers:** Elves, Centaurs
- **Associated Domains:** Arcana, Light, Nature

The Arch Heart appreciated beauty and talent in every form, and was particularly proud of granting mortals magic and of their achievements using it, however questionable. This eventually caused some resentment from other gods, but the Arch Heart continued enjoying the charm and value of mortal magic.
