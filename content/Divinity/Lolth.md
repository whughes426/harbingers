### Quick Facts
- **Type:** Betrayer God
- **Title:** The Spider Queen
- **Province:** Deceit, spiders
- **Symbol:** Jeweled spider
- **Gender:** Feminine
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Evil
- **Home plane:** Abyss
- **Worshipers:** Much of Drow Society, except for the Kryn Dynasty
- **Associated Domains:** Knowledge, Trickery

In Wildemount, a Lolthite network of spies, assassins, and raiders called the Children of Malice works on behalf of Loth to undermine the Kryn Dynasty.
