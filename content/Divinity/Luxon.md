### Quick Facts
- **Type:** Lesser Idol
- **Title:** Light, Lord of Light, First Radiance, Lost Beacon of Unknown Light
- **Province:** Light, Rebirth, Possibility
- **Symbol:** Hollow dodecahedron
- **Gender:** Genderless
- **Alignment:** Neutral
- **Home plane:** Arborea
- **Worshipers:** Kryn Dynasty
- **Associated Domains:** Arcana, Light

According to the creation myth believed by Kryn Dynasty, a single Light emerged from the darkness at the creation point of the universe. Other lights formed around this Light, but those lights settled as stars; the single Light, which was later called the Luxon, resisted that urge to burn and instead traveled, seeking self-knowledge.

The Luxon, having noticed a particular lonely planet, "a cold black ball of nothing in the cosmos" that was inhospitable to life, broke itself apart, embraced the world and gave life and light to it.  The Kryn believe that the Luxon now rests until it can be brought back together.

The **Luxon beacons** are sacred relics of the Kryn Dynasty, which they believe to be fragments of the Luxon's physical form. These relics are critical to the Dynasty's study of dunamancy and the practice of consecution.
