### Quick Facts
- **Type:** Prime Deity
- **Title:** Matron of Death, Matron of raven, Duskmaven
- **Province:** Death, Fate, Winter, Twilight, Inevitability
- **Symbol:** White, humanoid mask framed in black feathers
- **Gender:** Feminine
- **Alignment:** Lawful neutral
- **Home plane:** Shadowfell

Not long after her ascension, the Matron of Ravens made Letherna her realm in the Shadowfell. She became revered by the shadar-kai, elves that now lived in that plane and who received new powers thanks to the goddess; thus she gained servitors that followed her not only out of fear, but out of respect and love, and her cult extended both in the Shadow Realm and in the Material Plane.
